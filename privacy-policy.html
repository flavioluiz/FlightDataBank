Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 10, 2024

Introduction

Welcome to FlightDataBank. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy will inform you about how we look after your personal data when you visit our website and tell you about your privacy rights and how the law protects you.

The Data We Collect

When you visit FlightDataBank, we collect anonymous usage data through GoatCounter analytics. This includes:

  • Pages visited
  • Referrer information (where you came from)
  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Screen size
  • Country of origin (based on IP address)

We do not collect personally identifiable information. Our analytics platform (GoatCounter) is privacy-focused and does not use cookies or track users across websites.

How We Use Your Data

We use the anonymous usage data solely for the purpose of:

  • Understanding how visitors use our website
  • Improving our content and user experience
  • Analyzing which features are most popular
  • Identifying technical issues

Third-Party Services

Our website uses the following third-party services:

  • GoatCounter - For privacy-friendly analytics
  • GitHub Pages - For website hosting
  • Bootstrap CDN - For loading CSS frameworks
  • Font Awesome CDN - For loading icons

These services may collect some information as described in their respective privacy policies.

Data Retention

Anonymous analytics data is retained for a maximum of 6 months.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your data, including:

  • The right to access
  • The right to rectification
  • The right to erasure
  • The right to restrict processing
  • The right to data portability
  • The right to object

Since we do not collect personally identifiable information, these rights may have limited applicability.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page and updating the "last updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through the GitHub repository or visit the author's website.
